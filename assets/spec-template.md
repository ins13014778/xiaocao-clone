# Implementation Spec Template

## Goal

What is being built or fixed?

## Scope

What is included and excluded?

## Architecture

What components or modules are involved?

## Interfaces

What inputs, outputs, APIs, events, or contracts matter?

## Rules

What business rules, technical constraints, or coding norms must be followed?

## Risks

What could go wrong?

## Verification

How will we confirm the work is correct?
